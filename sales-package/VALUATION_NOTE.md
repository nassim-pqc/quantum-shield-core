## Factors That Reduce Price

| Factor | Impact | Explanation |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Zero revenue | High | No market validation |
| Zero customers | High | No production proof |
| No independent audit | High | Crypto audit required before production |
| Single developer | Medium | Bus factor risk |
| Emerging PQC market | Medium | Market still forming |
| Complex KMS integration | Low | Enterprise license model needed |
| No SOC 2 / ISO 27001 | Medium | Required for enterprise sales |

---

## Recommended Approach

1. **Start with Scenario 1 or 2** — Quick or reasonable acquisition
2. **Negotiate based on due diligence findings** — Audit results, code quality
3. **Consider earnout** — Part upfront, part on milestones (POC, customers, revenue)
4. **Include integration support** — 30-90 days of transition assistance
5. **Plan for audit** — Buyer should budget $30K-$80K for independent crypto audit
